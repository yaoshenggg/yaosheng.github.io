Biography

    Sheng received the B.Eng. and Ph.D. degrees in mechatronics engineering from South China University of Technology, Guangzhou, China, in 2016 and 2021, respectively, under Prof. Xianmin Zhang.

    From 2018 to 2019, he was a joint Ph.D. student in the Nonlinear Systems Control Laboratory, University of Toronto, Toronto, ON, Canada, supervised by Prof. James K. Mills. Since 2021, he has joined the faculty of Biomedical Engineering as a lecturer at Southern Medical University, China. His research interests include high-precision vision system and vision-based control for micromanipulation, with the applications to cell surgery.
